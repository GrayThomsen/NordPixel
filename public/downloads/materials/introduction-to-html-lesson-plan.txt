Introduction to HTML - Lesson Plan

- Audience: 11-14 years
- Subject: Web Development
- Duration: 12 pages
- Goal: Introduce semantic HTML, document structure, links, and images.

Suggested lesson flow:
1. Explain what HTML is and how browsers read it.
2. Build a simple page with headings, paragraphs, and lists.
3. Add links and images.
4. Review accessibility basics and clean structure.
